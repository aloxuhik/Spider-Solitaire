PlayingCards Pack

This package include playing cards asset.

- It comes with 52 playing cards, 2 jokers, white blank cards, and 25 types of other items that help you create games. 
- You can choose the backside color of playing cards from 3 different materials (red, blue, and black).
- It also contains 4 types of male & female motions, a total of 8 types of playing cards motions corresponding to the models from GameAssetStudio.
